Frequently Asked Questions

What makes your cookies keto-friendly?

Our cookies are made with zero sugar and ultra-low carb ingredients like almond flour, coconut flour, and natural sweeteners such as erythritol and monk fruit. This means you can enjoy a delicious treat without the blood sugar spike.

How does the monthly drop work?

Once a month, we release a limited batch of our curated cookie boxes. To get one, you must place your order during the "drop" period, which typically lasts a few days or until we sell out. Joining our email list is the best way to get notified so you don't miss it!

What are the macros for the cookies?

Each cookie's nutritional information varies slightly by flavor, but they are all designed to be keto-friendly. Generally, each cookie has between 2-4g of net carbs. Detailed nutritional information for the current month's flavors can be found on the product details page.

How should I store my cookies?

For optimal freshness, we recommend storing your cookies in an airtight container at room temperature for up to 5 days. For longer storage, they can be refrigerated for up to 2 weeks or frozen for up to 3 months.
